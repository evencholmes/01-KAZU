ABOUT KAZU

KAZU v2.0 — Money OS

This is a pet project — built by one person, for personal use, shared freely. It is not a commercial product. There is no support team, no SLA, no guarantees.

All data is stored on your device only. Nothing is ever sent to a server. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.

KAZU includes a basic app lock (PIN or pattern) but it is not a security product — it is a convenience feature to keep casual eyes out. For sensitive financial data, use your device's own screen lock as the primary protection.

Export a .kazu backup regularly to keep your data safe.
