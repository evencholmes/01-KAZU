# 01 KAZU
 Monthly budget tracker

Hey I made KAZU because every money tracking app I tried either had ads, a paywall, or wanted my data. I wanted none of that. So I spent a whole 2 day build this.

It's called KAZU. It tracks your money. Everything stays on your device no accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Just a simple app that does its job and leaves you alone.

I'm not putting it on the Play Store. Honestly? It costs $25 to upload an app there, and I don't have that right now. I make about $30 a month from my internship. And even if I did have it why bother? This is just a pet project. I built it for myself, my friend saw it and found it useful, so I'm sharing it. That's it.

If you use it and think it's cool a dollar would genuinely mean a lot. Not because you owe me anything, only if you want to and only if you can. There are a few other apps on here too, same deal offline, no ads, no nonsense.

Built with obsession. Free by choice.
